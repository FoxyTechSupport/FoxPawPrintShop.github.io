VINYL STICKERS

These are die cut vinyl stickers made by layering cut vinyl onto a backing. This backing means they are like regular printed stickers, you just peel and stick. The result of making them this way is a high quality sticker with vibrant colours, that can be applied to a wide array of surfaces, is weatherproof, resistant to damage, and the adhesion can be improved through heat.
