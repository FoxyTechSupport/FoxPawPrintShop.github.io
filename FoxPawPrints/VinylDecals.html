Vinyl Sticker

These are vinyl cut stickers, meaning it is cut from a sheet of vinyl and processed by hand into the finished product. The result is a high quality sticker, that can be applied to a wide array of surfaces, is weatherproof, resistant to damage, has no background and adhesion can be improved through heat.

Foxpaw print vinyl cut decals look great on notepads, water bottles, cars, lamp posts and even under bridges!!!
