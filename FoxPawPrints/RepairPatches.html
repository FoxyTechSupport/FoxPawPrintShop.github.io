Printed Repair Patches

In this time of fast fashion and low quality materials, as well as the increase in prices and cost of living, it can be important to make good clothes last. A simple way to repair holes or weakened fabric is with a sew-on patch! These patches come in 3 sizes and allow you to easily repair clothes as well as add an element of style and customisation with vinyl printed

All patches are hand made, out of 100% recycled fabrics with the goal of keeping things sustainable and high quality. Each fabric that you can buy a patch in has a strength rating, this is based on elements like how easily it is ripped or pierced as well as how much it takes to wear through it
