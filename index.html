ABOUT



Hello and welcome to the FoxPaw-PrintShop! A small individually run business based in Northern Ireland!
Given this shop is individually run I have many roles and titles, however it is effectively a way for me to improve my graphic design and art, as well as functioning as a creative project; manufacturing the majority of products myself.
There is a heavy focus on products that allow you to express yourself and leave your pawprint in places (getting the name yet?). This goal of self expression is a large inspiration for the designs, no corporate santisation, swears and sex references are fully allowed!
There is also a large focus on durability, stickers made to stay stuck down and not get damaged by the elements, clothing designed to last years of heavy frequent wear etc!

This will probably be more indepth in the future, so stay tuned!
